# The Office

Thirty-two status messages from the Scranton branch of Dunder Mifflin. The whole ensemble shows up:
Michael's cringe and the Dundies, Dwight's beets and "fact", Jim's pranks and that glance at the camera,
Kevin's chili, Stanley's crossword, Pretzel Day. For anyone who quotes this show at work without
realizing it.

Pairs well with `replace` mode, and with a glance at the camera every time a test fails.

By [@Krythz43](https://github.com/Krythz43).

> Fan made theme. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by NBCUniversal or the creators of The
> Office. Quotes and references are fan tribute.
