# Code of conduct

This is a small, friendly project. The rules are short.

- Be respectful. No harassment, hate, or personal attacks.
- Keep theme content in good taste: nothing hateful, harassing, or NSFW.
- Assume good faith in reviews and discussion. Critique the work, not the person.
- Respect trademarks and copyright. Fan themes are welcome with a clear "not affiliated" note.

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove contributions that break these rules. To report a problem, open a
private report through the repository's GitHub issues, or contact a maintainer listed in a theme you are
concerned about.
